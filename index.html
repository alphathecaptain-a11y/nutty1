7. Launch Phases - Revised Structure

Phase-Based Fee Distribution Strategy

A balanced approach that ensures both NUTTY and PNUT ecosystems thrive while building a professional DEX.

Token Emission Model

Community token emissions follow a time-based linear vesting schedule over 72 months. During early launch phases, emission rates are increased to bootstrap liquidity and incentivize early adoption. All emissions are distributed proportionally to:

  • NLP providers and stakers based on their pool shares
  • Active traders based on trading volume and fees paid
  • PNUT holders who stake in the dedicated vault

Note: Complex performance-based reward mechanisms may be introduced in future phases based on community governance.

Phase 1: Genesis & Bootstrap (Months 1-3)

Objective: Build liquidity while supporting both ecosystems

DEX Trading Fee Distribution:

  • 40% ‚Üí NLP Providers (essential liquidity)
  • 20% ‚Üí PNUT Buybacks (partnership value)
  • 20% ‚Üí NUTTY Buybacks (immediate utility)
  • 10% ‚Üí Insurance Fund (user protection)
  • 10% ‚Üí Development & Operations
