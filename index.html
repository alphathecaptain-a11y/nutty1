3. NUTTY Liquidity Provider Token (NLP)

Integration with Orderly Network

NUTTYDex leverages Orderly Network's Omnivault infrastructure to power the NLP mechanism. Orderly's Omnivault operates similarly to HLP (Hyperliquid Liquidity Provider) pools, providing:

  • Cross-margin perpetual trading liquidity
  • Automated market-making across all trading pairs
  • Unified liquidity pool serving as counterparty to traders
  • Professional risk management and position hedging

NLP wraps Orderly's Omnivault with NUTTY-specific features: additional NUTTY token emissions, PNUT holder boosts, and NUTTYDex fee rebates.

How NLP Works

  • Users deposit USDC or other accepted assets to mint NLP tokens
  • Deposits flow into Orderly's Omnivault infrastructure
  • NLP acts as the counterparty to all perpetual trades on NUTTYDex
  • Value accrues from trading fees, funding rate profits, and liquidation proceeds
  • NLP holders earn base APY from Orderly + bonus NUTTY emissions

Scoring Formula

Trader Score =

Σ [(Ktaker × VolumeBTC/ETH × Bpair × Bloyalty)b1]
+ Σ [(Ktaker × Volumealts × Bpair × Bloyalty)b2]
+ Σ [(Kmaker × VolumeBTC/ETH × Bpair × Bloyalty)b3]
+ Σ [(Kmaker × Volumealts × Bpair × Bloyalty)b4]

Formula Parameters

Parameter Value Description
Ktaker 0.0014 Taker multiplier (4× maker rate)
Kmaker 0.00035 Maker multiplier (incentivizes liquidity)
b1 (BTC/ETH maker) 0.80 Volume exponent - favors makers
b2 (BTC/ETH taker) 0.83 Volume exponent for takers
b3 (Alt maker) 0.82 Alt pair maker exponent
b4 (Alt taker) 0.85 Alt pair taker exponent
c 0.7 Global dampening constant

Points Distribution

How Weekly Points Are Allocated:

  1. Calculate individual scores using the formula above for each trader
  2. Sum all trader scores: total_users_score = Σ(trader_score)
  3. Normalize to weekly cap:
    user_points = (user_score ÷ total_users_score) × weekly_cap

Example Distribution (2M weekly cap):

  • Top 1 trader: ~100k-150k points (5-7.5% max)
  • Top 10 traders: ~600k points total (30%)
  • Top 50 traders: ~1M points total (50%)
  • Top 100 traders: ~1.4M points total (70%)
  • All other traders: ~600k points (30%)

Distribution designed to prevent whale dominance and encourage broad community participation.

Points to Tokens Conversion

How Your Points Convert to NUTTY Tokens at TGE:

Total Points Pool (6 weeks):

Week 1-2: 1,500,000 × 2 = 3,000,000 points
Week 3-4: 2,000,000 × 2 = 4,000,000 points
Week 5-6: 3,000,000 × 2 = 6,000,000 points
Total: 13,000,000 points

TGE Airdrop Allocation:

53,500,000 NUTTY tokens (10% of Community allocation)

Conversion Formula:

Your Tokens = (Your Total Points ÷ 13,000,000) × 53,500,000

Example Calculation:

If you earn 65,000 points across all 6 weeks:
Your Tokens = (65,000 ÷ 13,000,000) × 53,500,000
Your Tokens = 267,500 NUTTY
